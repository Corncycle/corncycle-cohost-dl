Quilynn@qln11/25/2022, 9:20 PM

Hi, I noticed something with the way you're using "aria-hidden="true"" to hide the spliced up text from screen readers. It's not properly hiding because the attribute is assigned to a span element immediately before a paragraph element where the stylized text is. Since you can't have a paragraph within a span, the span breaks and you lose the aria-hidden attribute, and all the individual characters might be read out loud.

It works fine if you just get rid of the < p > tags. You could also put the < p > tags around everything, but I think if you just remove them completely then it'll work to let people use markdown plus on only small bits of text within a paragraph which would be awesome. I tried looking through the js files I can see on the site, for where it's inserting a < p > but since I know next to nothing about js I got nowhere
